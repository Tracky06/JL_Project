About JL Properties

The City's Favourite Developer. We are geared towards providing customers with time-tested residential homes at affordable prices without compromising on quality and luxury.

The City's Favorite Developer

JL Properties, an Accra - based multi-purpose organization, was incorporated in 2011 with excellent financial, project and strategic management. The company's core services include; Building & Road Construction, Civil Engineering works and Real Estate Development.

JL Properties' clientele includes multinationals, public listed companies, retail customers, high net worth individuals in Ghana or who seek to invest here, the embassies and high our biggest asset.

Our Mission

At JL Properties, we are geared towards providing customers with time-tested residential homes at affordable prices without compromising on quality and luxury.

Our Objective

To be the leading real estate firm in Africa offering our services at the best affordable prices without compromising on quality and luxury.

Our Vision

To be the most preferred real estate firm in Ghana and beyond through continuous investment in valuable residential and commercial houses.

Value for Life
