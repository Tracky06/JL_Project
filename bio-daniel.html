Mr Daniel Ansah - Obuobi

General Manager (Finance)

Biography

He is the General Manager (Finance) of JL Properties. Daniel has over 16 years’ experience in the banking sector. He began his professional carrier with the then Metropolitan and Allied Bank as Operations Officer from 2001 to 2004. He also held the position of Operations Manager/ Branch Manager from 2004-2008 at UBA Ghana. He also worked at CAL Bank, Branch Management and Risk Department at the Senior Level Position (2009-2015). Immediately before joining JL Properties Limited Mr. Daniel Obuobi worked at Beige Capital as Regional Credit Risk Manager from 2016 to 2017 and as well Private Business Consultant in 2018.

He holds MBA in Banking and Finance from the School Business, Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology, Kumasi. He also holds Bachelor of Art in   Economics and Statistics combined from the University of Ghana-Legon. . Besides the banking carrier, he has also consulted for a number of businesses in the areas of Management and Financial Advisory Services. He has strength in Business Restructuring, Financial Advisory, Risk Management and Business Negotiations.

As predominant real estate developers, we have over the years played and continue to take a major role in the expansion of the country's housing and infrastructural needs.
