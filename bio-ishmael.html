Mr. Ishmael Faustinus Commey

Director  Marketing and Operations

Biography

He is the GM  Marketing and Sales  of JL Properties Limited.   He was previously in charge of the company’s project operations.  He joined the company in 2017 as the project and site coordinator. After one year as the project coordinator, Commey was promoted to GM operations holds due to his handwork, servant leadership and humility. Commey is versatile knowledgeable and has the ability to coordinate and handle multitask effectively and efficiently. Prior to joining JL Properties Limited, Commey served as a Senior Course Tutor at the College of Distance Education from University of Cape Coast. He has also been a course tutor of Mfantsipim School in Cape Coast. He is an Editor/Text Book Evaluator of Sam-Woode Publishers Limited.

Mr. Ishmael Faustinus Commey holds a Master of Philosophy degree in Educational Administration and a Bachelor of Education (Arts) Degree all from the University of Cape Coast.

As predominant real estate developers, we have over the years played and continue to take a major role in the expansion of the country's housing and infrastructural needs.
