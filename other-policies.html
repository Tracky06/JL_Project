Other Policies

In addition to our Privacy Policy and Terms of Use, JL Properties follows these additional policies to ensure transparency and fairness in our operations:

1. Refund Policy

JL Properties does not generally offer refunds for deposits, application fees, or other payments made for property leasing or purchasing unless explicitly stated in a contract. Any requests for refunds will be evaluated on a case-by-case basis, in compliance with the terms outlined in the agreement.

2. Fair Housing Policy

JL Properties is committed to providing equal housing opportunities to all individuals, regardless of race, color, religion, national origin, gender, familial status, disability, or any other characteristic protected by law. We strictly adhere to all applicable fair housing laws.

3. Maintenance Policy

Our properties are maintained to the highest standards. Tenants and homeowners can report maintenance issues through our designated channels, and we will respond promptly. Routine maintenance schedules and emergency repairs are outlined in individual leasing or purchase agreements.

4. Environmental Policy

At JL Properties, we are committed to sustainable development and environmentally conscious practices. We strive to reduce our environmental impact through eco-friendly building materials, energy-efficient systems, and waste reduction efforts across all our properties

As predominant real estate developers, we have over the years played and continue to take a major role in the expansion of the country's housing and infrastructural needs.
